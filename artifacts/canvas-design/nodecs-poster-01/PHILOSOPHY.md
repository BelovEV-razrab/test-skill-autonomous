## NODECS Poster Philosophy

This poster follows an editorial Swiss-grid logic: strict margins, visible structure, and a restrained hierarchy where typography carries the primary message. The composition is intentionally quiet, with strong alignment and consistent spacing to maintain clarity at first glance.

The visual system uses a neutral paper-like background and dark ink typography, then introduces a narrow blue accent layer to signal technology without turning the layout into a noisy "tech wallpaper." The accent behaves as infrastructure, not decoration.

Text is reduced to essentials: one headline and one compact domain line. This keeps the message direct and brand-forward while preserving negative space as an active design element.

The result balances studio professionalism and automation energy: rational grid discipline from editorial design, plus subtle geometric traces that suggest systems, flow, and connected processes.
